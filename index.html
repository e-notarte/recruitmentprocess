Proposal

Automation on Business System

A centralized paperless recruitment management system using Google Workspace to modernize and simplify the company's recruitment workflow.

Prepared — May 2026 | en.dev

Executive Summary

This proposal aims to modernize and simplify the company's recruitment workflow through a centralized paperless recruitment management system. By leveraging Google Workspace and automation tools, we will eliminate manual processes, reduce paper usage, and create a streamlined hiring pipeline from application submission to employee onboarding.

Current Problems on Manual Application

  • Manual paper application
  • Difficult applicant tracking
  • Slow recruitment process
  • Risk of lost documents
  • Time-consuming encoding
  • Hard to monitor applicant status
  • Storage and printing cost

Proposed Solution

A centralized online recruitment system designed to automate and streamline the hiring process from application submission to hiring. This solution will use:

  • Online Application Portal
  • Email Notification
  • Applicant Tracking Dashboard
  • Resume Database
  • Interview Scheduling
  • Status Monitoring
  • Hiring Reports
