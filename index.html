Proposal

Automation on Business System

A centralized and automated recruitment management solution powered by Google Workspace, designed to streamline hiring operations, improve applicant tracking, and enhance overall recruitment efficiency through a fully paperless workflow..

Prepared — May 2026 | en.dev

Summary

This proposal presents a modern recruitment management solution designed to transform the company’s hiring process into a centralized, automated, and paperless system. By leveraging Google Workspace technologies and workflow automation, the organization can significantly improve operational efficiency, reduce manual administrative tasks, enhance applicant monitoring, and establish a more organized and scalable recruitment process from application submission through employee onboarding.

Importance of Using Automation

⚙️

Reduce Manual Work

Automation minimizes repetitive administrative tasks, allowing HR personnel to focus on strategic decision-making, applicant engagement, and workforce development rather than manual encoding and document handling.

🚀

Improve Efficiency

Streamlined workflows and automated processes accelerate recruitment operations, reduce turnaround time, and improve coordination across the hiring process.

🌐

Google Workspace

Utilize integrated Google Workspace tools such as Gmail, Google Forms, Sheets, and Drive to create a seamless, secure, and collaborative recruitment environment.

Current Problems on Manual Application

  • Manual paper-based application processing
  • Difficulty in tracking applicant progress and records
  • Delayed recruitment and approval procedures
  • Risk of misplaced or incomplete documents
  • Time-consuming manual data encoding
  • Limited visibility of applicant status updates
  • Increasing printing, storage, and administrative costs

Proposed Solution

The proposed solution is a centralized web-based recruitment management system that automates and simplifies the company’s hiring operations. The platform will provide an organized digital workflow that enables efficient applicant processing, real-time monitoring, secure document management, and streamlined communication between HR personnel and applicants.

  • Online Applicant Submission Portal
  • Automated Email Notifications
  • Centralized Applicant Tracking Dashboard
  • Digital Resume and Document Database
  • Interview Scheduling and Monitoring
  • Real-Time Application Status Tracking
  • Recruitment Analytics and Hiring Reports
